About the AfRID Network

What we are doing

The AFRID Network are bringing together a number of peer African institutions and researchers that are willing to work together, with a view to generating a critical mass that could more effectively support development initiatives in the continent.

The AFRID Network is led by AAU for African Universities.

However, it is a grouping of Higher education institutions in Africa with excellence in the areas of each forum. International Collaboration – Obreal Global, etc
